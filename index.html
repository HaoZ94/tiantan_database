The Tiantan Hospital Database

Pioneering the frontiers of iEEG technology and innovation.

Project Roadmap

01

High-Quality Dataset

We will utilize single-center data from Beijing Tiantan Hospital to construct a high-quality, open-access epilepsy dataset comprising 50 cases. We anticipate that the establishment and sharing of this dataset will precisely advance the in-depth research on intracranial electroencephalographic (iEEG) mechanisms in epilepsy surgery, positioning it as a foundational research resource comparable to those from leading international centers.

02

Multi-Center Platform

We will collaborate with multiple top-tier epilepsy centers and research institutions across China to jointly develop a standardized iEEG data platform tailored for small-sample studies. This platform will integrate advanced paradigms such as neural navigation and semantic decoding, aiming to drive cutting-edge exploration in cognitive neuroscience, including brain-computer interface (BCI) research.

03

Blockchain Ecosystem

We will actively explore the use of consortium blockchain technology to establish a large-scale, multi-center iEEG platform for clinical and cognitive neuroscience research. Our objective is to facilitate the secure and compliant flow of data elements among interdisciplinary teams, fundamentally eliminating "data silos," and maximizing the scientific and clinical value of large-scale iEEG data while rigorously protecting patient privacy and data security.
